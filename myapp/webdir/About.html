About Our Company

TicGet was founded in April of 2018 in the campus Which Wich by a group of students with a vision of bringing affordable music experiences to the citizens of Tennessee. We offer low prices through a membership program sponsored by local venues in the Memphis, Knoxville, and Nashville areas. We hope to grow across the United States in order to bring extraordinary concerts and events to everybody.
